Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2, 2026

1. Overview

TickAuto is a personal tool that enables automated video posting to TikTok. This policy explains how the App handles your data.

2. Data We Collect

The App collects and stores locally: TikTok OAuth access token and refresh token, TikTok account Open ID, video file paths and captions you provide.

3. How We Use Your Data

Access tokens are used solely to authenticate API requests to TikTok on your behalf. No data is shared with third parties.

4. Data Storage

All data is stored locally on your own device. No data is transmitted to any external server other than TikTok's official API endpoints.
